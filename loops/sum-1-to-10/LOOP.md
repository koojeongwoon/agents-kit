# Sum 1 to 10 Loop Recipe

## Goal
Iteratively add numbers from 1 to 10 to a running total.

## Done Condition
- `current_number` reaches 11
- `sum` is calculated and verified as 55
- The step-by-step loop log is recorded in `memory.md`

## Procedure
1. Initialize `current_number` = 1, `sum` = 0.
2. In each iteration, add `current_number` to `sum`.
3. Increment `current_number` by 1.
4. Record the iteration in `memory.md`.
5. Repeat until `current_number` > 10.
